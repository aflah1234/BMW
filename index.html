it's BMW

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a German multinational company that produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Founded in 1916, BMW is known for its performance, innovation, and engineering excellence. The company's headquarters are in Munich, Germany, and it is part of the "German Big Three" luxury automakers, along with Audi and Mercedes-Benz.
